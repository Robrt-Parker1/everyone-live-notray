What is Notarization?

Notarization is a formal process performed by a notary public to verify the identity of the signer and their willingness to sign the document without duress. The notary ensures that the document is signed in their presence and that it complies with all necessary legal standards. Once verified, the notary places an official seal and signature on the document, making it legally recognized within the United States.

What is an Apostille Service?

An apostille is an additional layer of certification that verifies the authenticity of public documents for use in foreign countries that are part of the Hague Apostille Convention. An Apostille Service in Carson CA helps people validate their notarized or state-issued documents so that they can be legally recognized abroad. Unlike standard notarization, an apostille ensures international acceptance of the documents in question.

Key Differences in Usage

Notarization is generally required for domestic purposes such as real estate transactions, wills, or powers of attorney. Apostille services, on the other hand, are necessary when documents need to be recognized in another country. For example, if you are moving abroad or planning to conduct business in a foreign country, documents like your birth certificate, marriage license, or academic transcripts will require an apostille.
